# Interview Me

## Overview
When requirements are unclear, ask structured questions to extract the necessary information before building anything.

## The Interview Questions

### Project Basics
1. What is the project/feature you want to build?
2. Who will use it?
3. What problem does it solve?

### Technical Details
4. What platform or framework does it need to work with?
5. Are there specific performance requirements?
6. What are the security requirements?

### Scope & Constraints
7. What is the timeline?
8. What is the minimum viable version?
9. What is explicitly out of scope?

### Definition of Done
10. How will we know when it's finished?
11. What tests or verifications are needed?
12. Who needs to approve the final result?

## Approach
- Ask one question at a time
- Summarize understanding after each answer
- Confirm before moving to next topic
- Document answers in a spec

## Verification
- [ ] All 12 questions answered
- [ ] Requirements documented
- [ ] User confirmed understanding
- [ ] Clear definition of done exists
